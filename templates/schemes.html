Government Schemes for Farmers

Empowering Indian farmers with knowledge about schemes and financial support provided by the government.

Available Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A comprehensive crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Learn More
Soil Health Card Scheme

Helps farmers understand the health of their soil and provides recommendations for nutrients and fertilizers.

Learn More
PM-KISAN

Offers financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year to farmers in three installments to support their agricultural needs.

Learn More
Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Encourages states to increase public investment in agriculture and allied sectors.

Learn More
National Agriculture Market (eNAM)

A digital platform that connects farmers with buyers, ensuring better price discovery and transparency in transactions.

Learn More
PM-KUSUM

A scheme that aims to promote the generation of solar power by farmers, reducing their electricity bills and making them energy-efficient.

Learn More

Frequently Asked Questions

The PM-KISAN scheme offers financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year to farmers in three installments to support their agricultural needs.
You can apply for the Soil Health Card Scheme by visiting the official website of the Ministry of Agriculture and filling out the application form online. You can also apply at the local agricultural office in your area.
The eNAM scheme allows farmers to sell their produce online through a national electronic platform, ensuring better price discovery and transparency in transactions.
You can check your eligibility for the PMFBY scheme on the official website or by visiting the nearest insurance office. The eligibility criteria are based on the type of crops grown, your region, and other factors.
